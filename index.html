Our Story

Burney's Hot Chicken story begins in the southern states of Tennessee, where my family roots are deeply embedded. Southern cooking was a cherished tradition at family gatherings, filled with flavors that brought us together. Among these dishes, one stood out—the Nashville-Style Hot Chicken sandwich. It quickly became my all-time favorite, shared and enjoyed with loved ones over the years.

When I moved to Honolulu, I found myself longing for that familiar taste but discovered there wasn’t a place dedicated solely to this iconic sandwich. So, I took matters into my own hands. After perfecting the recipe and sharing it with friends and family, the overwhelming response inspired me to bring a taste of the mainland to the islands. Thus, the World’s Smallest Hot Chicken Stand was born, right here in Honolulu, offering the community a flavor I had been missing and couldn’t wait to share.
