OUR STORY

Born from Passion,
Fueled by Fire

Burney's Hot Chicken brings the authentic soul of Nashville-style hot chicken straight to Honolulu. Every piece is hand-battered, fried to order, and coated in our signature spice blend — no shortcuts, no compromises.

From our famous chicken sandwiches and crispy tenders to our loaded fries and homemade desserts, every item on the menu is made with love and a whole lot of heat.

Located at 2105 S. King St in Honolulu, we're proud to be your neighborhood spot for bold flavors and real hospitality. Come hungry — and don't forget to order the Volcano Waffle.
